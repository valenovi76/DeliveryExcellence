Our Projects So Far

2019

Parked Orders

Reduction of the Orders Parked volume within the Delivery process, from 30% of our Work In Progress number to 5%. The improvement was achieved analysing the root cause of the issue: customer not ready for install. We removed the problem introducing a specific check list to be filled in by our representatives at the contract entry stage, after conducting a through analysis of the data at hand.

Cancellation Orders

60% Reduction of the Cancellation Orders backlog. This was obtained re-aligning the orders process to a new team structure. The performance focus has also been shifted to single step SLAs with stringent KPIs targets for the individuals. This more granular approach ensured targets could be met more easily, raising morale and improving the end result.

CI Silver Accreditation - Delivery Department

The Department succesfully past the second phase of the CI Accreditation path. The Silver accreditation proves that a customer-centric vision and strategy direction is effectively cascaded to all levels within the department and our managers ensure teams are engaged and working towards the same company strategy.

2018

Lead Time Reduction For Orders' Delivery

The lead-time reduction is a two-phased project that focuses on the reduction of our product delivery lead-times for our customers. The project uses the DMAIC methodology by mapping and reviewing the performance of the existing process to identity the issues with the delivery of each product. The processes and delivery systems will be re-engineered to exclude any wasteful activities. Phase 1 focuses on modify type orders (quick connects), while phase 2 will focus on On-Net products, Off Net and third party deliveries.

CI Bronze Accreditation - Delivery Department

CI accreditation is a formal assessment of the CI maturity level of a team. The entire department, comprising of four teams, underwent an external assessment, getting awarded a Bronze Certification.

2017

Order Validation Process Review

When completing the end to end process review it became apparent that every team during the delivery process felt the need to complete a validation of information provided to them or of work carried out ahead of their step, before they could complete their task. This was causing delays and inefficiencies in our processes. The blocker has been removed consolidating the data validation in one team and one step. The overall performance, measured in a reduction of invalid orders, has improved by over 30%.
