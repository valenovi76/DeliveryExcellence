Lead Time Reduction for orders' delivery

","

The lead-time reduction is a 2 phased project that focuses on the reduction of our Product delivery Lead-times for our customers. The project uses the DMAIC methodology by mapping and reviewing the performance of the existing process to identity the issues with the delivery of each Product. The processes and delivery systems will be re-engineered to exclude any wasteful activities. Phase 1 focuses on modify type orders (quick connects) while phase 2 will focus on BT on net, Off Net and third Party deliveries.","
